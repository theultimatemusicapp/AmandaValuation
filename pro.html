Step 1: Select Valuation Methods

Select up to three valuation methods to evaluate your SaaS business. Each method uses different financial metrics to estimate your company’s worth. Learn more

Multiplies your Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) by a factor (5–8) based on growth, churn, and market conditions. Ideal for stable revenue streams.
Example: $1M ARR with a 6x multiplier yields $6M.
Uses net profit or EBITDA, multiplied by a factor (4–7). Suitable for profitable businesses with strong margins.
Example: $200K profit with a 5x multiplier results in $1M.
Combines ARR and profit, adjusted for growth and retention. Best for rapidly scaling businesses.
Example: $1M ARR + $200K profit may yield $1.5M.
Projects future cash flows and discounts to present value. Ideal for businesses with long-term growth potential.
Example: $1M ARR with 20% growth may yield $3M.

Select methods that reflect your business’s financial strengths.
