What Multiple Do SaaS Businesses Sell For in 2025?

A snapshot of 2025 SaaS valuation multiples and the levers that move your business up the curve.

Check Your Multiple See Multiples Guide

2025 Market Snapshot

Based on reported broker data and private deals:

  • Sub-$1M ARR: 2.0x–4.5x ARR depending on churn, concentration, and margin.
  • $1M–$5M ARR: 3.5x–6.5x ARR with healthy growth and diversified channels.
  • Growth-stage with profit: 5x–9x ARR when net retention is strong and burn is low.
  • VC-backed with burn: Revenue multiples compress unless you post 40%+ growth.

Multipliers Buyers Reward

  • Net revenue retention above 100%.
  • Gross margin over 75% with low support costs.
  • Diverse acquisition mix (paid, organic, partner, outbound).
  • Documented handoff and minimal key-person risk.

Factors That Compress Multiples

  • Churn above 6–8% monthly or heavy logo churn in SMB.
  • Single integration partner accounting for >30% of revenue.
  • Large services component that lowers gross margin.
  • Unclear contracts or unpaid invoices over 60 days.

Quick Wins to Move Up

  • Convert monthly users to annual plans before marketing the sale.
  • Increase ARPU with add-ons or tiered limits.
  • Strengthen security posture (SSO, audit logs, SOC-lite policies).
  • Package clean cohort charts in your data room.

How to Position Your Ask

Buyers benchmark against similar transactions. Anchor your multiple with proof:

For Profitable SaaS

  • Show trailing twelve months SDE and normalized adjustments.
  • Share retention cohorts and payback to prove durability.
  • Offer a short seller note only if needed to close the gap.

For Growth-First SaaS

  • Highlight efficiency metrics (Rule of 40, burn multiple).
  • Map your pipeline, win rates, and sales capacity plan.
  • Share a 12–18 month roadmap tied to revenue milestones.
