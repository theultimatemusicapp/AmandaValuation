How our methodology works

  • Triangulate values across ARR multiples, efficiency metrics (Rule of 40, burn multiple), and profitability benchmarks.
  • Discount or uplift based on retention quality, concentration risk, customer acquisition payback, and revenue mix.
  • Layer qualitative scores (team redundancy, roadmap clarity, security posture) that buyers flag during diligence.
  • Translate outputs into investor-ready narratives for both cash-flow and strategic buyers.

Data sources

  • Private SaaS transaction comps from brokered deals, PE bids, and founder-led exits across $500K–$20M ARR.
  • Operational benchmarks pulled from anonymized cohort data (LTV/CAC, NRR, ARPU trends, gross margin bands).
  • Public market signals for relevant ARR and revenue multiples adjusted for growth, efficiency, and market risk.
  • Real-time user inputs inside the valuation product to keep heuristics aligned with current buyer questions.
