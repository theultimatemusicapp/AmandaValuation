Step 1: Select Valuation Methods

Choose up to 3 valuation methods that best fit your SaaS business. Learn more

Multiplies ARR by a factor (e.g., 5-8), ideal for steady recurring revenue or total earnings over the last 12 months.
Example: If ARR is $1,000,000 and multiplier is 6, valuation = $6,000,000.
Uses net profit times a multiple to highlight profitability.
Example: If net profit is $300,000 with a multiple of 5, valuation = $1,500,000.
Combines revenue, assets, and debt for asset-inclusive valuation.
Example: Adjusted revenue plus assets minus debt.
Projects future cash flows discounted to present value; ideal for predictable revenue.
Example: Present value of projected cash flow over 5 years.

Please select between 1 and 3 valuation methods.
